Ridley Scott Really Has No Time for French Critics of Napoleon

Ridley Scott has faced criticism from French critics over his latest movie "Napoleon," which stars Joaquin Phoenix as the titular character. In response to the negative feedback, Scott has been dismissive, particularly of historians questioning the film's accuracy, famously retorting that unless someone was present during the events, they should remain quiet. Scott has also advised those fixated on inaccuracies to "get a life" and appears to be gaining attention for his candid comments during the movie's promotion. Joaquin Phoenix suggests that while the film presents Napoleon through Ridley Scott's vision, those interested in the historical figure should research on their own to truly understand him.
